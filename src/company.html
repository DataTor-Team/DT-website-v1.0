About

DataTor is not a yet-another-data-application startup instead we provide data privacy through our products and data enabling services to OEMs, connectivity providers, 3rd party services, and in-turn to car users.

Having started small with just a team of four aspiring engineers, we successfully managed to reach pilots and prove our technology meets the industry standards, with more developments and plans in the pipeline - progressing with MobilityXLab and company partners will help us gain the readiness level nimbler to production. To put more light onto the context of the solution scope and deployment scope of our first product, putting them on X and Y axis could land DataTor in the fourth quadrant with more potential for the deployment aspects wherein the problem scope is covering most of the pain that is addressed within the Connected Car domain.
