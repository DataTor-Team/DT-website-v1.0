How Datator brings Privacy to you?

Due to high connectivity between devices & services, the question of what we are sharing , to whom and the control of that are a puzzle to both user and businesses - creating privacy concerns. The need from the general fair connected data market is to have a fair and more transparent consentful data communications.


DataTor operates with unique privacy framework and intuitive design, thereby bringing data awareness and control to the device user. We do that without any intervention to the connection and without reading any user data payloads. It in-turn facilitate a consentful data collection to OEM and act as a privacy gateway for 3rd party services adding intelligence in every levels.All we do is putting you in an elegant state of control with our intelligence - Privacy is rest assured for all !!
